A: There are several useful visual features to tell there is an osprey in a photo:
- large, white-breasted bird with brown wings
- long tail extending beyond tip of wings when perched or in flight
- usually seen flying or hovering high above water

- medium sized bird
- blue or purple body, light green wing patches, pink face, dark bill, and black eyes
